How It Works

The Format
  • Salary cap pool across 5 tournaments: The Players, Masters, PGA Championship, U.S. Open, and The Open
  • Pick 6 golfers per event — stay under the salary cap
  • Only the best 4 of 6 scores count each tournament
  • Golfers are one-and-done — once picked, they can't be used again all season
  • Multiple entries allowed per person
Scoring
  • Total strokes — lowest cumulative score wins
  • Missed cut / WD / incomplete round = highest (worst) score of that day
  • Tiebreaker: predict the winner's total strokes (e.g. 275)
